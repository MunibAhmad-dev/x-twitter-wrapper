Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 24, 2026

Apps for TikTok ("we", "our", or "us") is an independent macOS productivity application distributed through the Mac App Store. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum data necessary to operate the app:

  • In-App Purchase records — processed entirely by Apple. We never see your payment details.
  • App preferences — theme, sidebar state, notification settings — stored locally on your device only.
  • Workspace configuration — account labels and workspace names you create — stored locally on your device only.

We do not collect your name, email address, TikTok credentials, or any personally identifiable information.

2. TikTok

Apps for TikTok is an independent tool and is not affiliated with TikTok Inc. or ByteDance Ltd. The app displays TikTok.com inside a native macOS window. Your TikTok login, messages, and all account activity are governed by TikTok's own Privacy Policy and Terms of Service. TikTok® is a trademark of TikTok Inc.

3. AI Features

When you use the AI Reply, Translation, or AI Post Studio features, the text you submit is sent to OpenAI's API to generate a response. Only the text you explicitly submit is sent — no other data leaves your device. OpenAI's data handling is governed by their Privacy Policy.

4. Data Storage

All app data (preferences, workspaces, subscription status) is stored locally on your Mac using macOS system storage. Nothing is uploaded to our servers. We do not operate any backend servers or databases that store user data.

5. In-App Purchases

Subscriptions and one-time purchases are handled entirely by Apple via the Mac App Store. We do not process, store, or have access to your payment information. You can manage or cancel subscriptions at any time from App Store → Account → Subscriptions.

6. Analytics & Tracking

We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking tools. We do not track your usage, clicks, or behavior within the app.

7. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
