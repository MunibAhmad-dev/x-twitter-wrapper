Top Priority

Last updated: July 5, 2026  ·  Apps for X  ·  Version 1.0

Apps for X is a native macOS desktop application that provides a convenient multi-account wrapper for X (Twitter). This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights as a user. We believe in full transparency — Apps for X does not collect, store, or sell any personal information.

🔍

1. Information We Collect

Apps for X is designed with privacy-first principles. We collect no personal information whatsoever.

  • We do not collect your name, email, or contact details
  • We do not collect X (Twitter) login credentials
  • We do not read, store, or transmit your messages or posts
  • We do not collect device identifiers or IP addresses
  • We do not use analytics, crash reporting, or telemetry services
  • We do not track your activity inside the app

How X authentication works: When you sign into X within the app, your credentials are entered directly into X's official website (x.com) loaded inside a secure browser view. Apps for X never sees or intercepts your password.

💾

2. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

Apps for X stores the following data exclusively on your Mac, never on remote servers:

  • Account labels and workspace names you create inside the app
  • App preferences (theme, sidebar state, notifications toggle)
  • Browser session cookies managed by macOS's built-in WKWebView / Electron session — identical to a standard browser

All local data can be deleted at any time by removing the app from your Mac (via Finder → Applications → Move to Trash).

🤖

3. AI-Powered Features

Apps for X includes optional AI tools: AI Reply Assistant, AI Thread Generator, and AI Tweet Optimizer. These features are powered by OpenAI's API.

When you use an AI feature, the text you enter (e.g. a tweet to optimize or a topic for a thread) is sent to OpenAI's servers to generate a response. Please review OpenAI's Privacy Policy for details on how they handle API data.

  • AI features are opt-in — you must actively click a button to use them
  • No AI requests are made automatically or in the background
  • We do not store or log the text you send to AI features
🌐

4. Third-Party Services

Apps for X embeds the official X (Twitter) website. All interactions with X — including login, posts, and messages — are governed by X's own privacy policy and terms of service:

  • X (Twitter) Privacy Policy
  • X (Twitter) Terms of Service

Apps for X is an independent product and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by X Corp. or Twitter, Inc.

🔐

5. App Permissions & Entitlements

Apps for X requests only the minimum permissions required to function:

  • Network access — to load x.com in the browser view
  • User-selected file access — only when you explicitly choose to download a file or image from X

Apps for X does not request access to your camera, microphone, location, contacts, calendar, photos library, or any other sensitive system resource.

👶

6. Children's Privacy

Apps for X is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. The app requires a valid X (Twitter) account, which itself requires users to be at least 13 years of age.

📝

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date at the top. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

We encourage you to review this page periodically to stay informed about how we protect your privacy.
